# UrjaNetra AI — 3-Minute Demo Script

This script outlines the chronological flow for presenting UrjaNetra AI. The demo highlights the transition from a baseline state to a critical energy crisis, showing how the backend engines optimize national energy security in real-time.

---

### **0:00 - 0:30 | Command Center & Crisis Activation**
1. **Command Center**:
   - Start on `/command-center`.
   - Point out the **India Energy Risk Map** (with live refinery and port nodes) and key baseline metrics (e.g., National Risk Score, SPR Coverage).
   - Explain: *"This is the default baseline state. The system shows normal risk levels, secure supplies, and steady domestic energy prices."*
2. **Upload/Activate Crisis**:
   - Click the **Upload Crisis Feed** button in the Command Center header.
   - Show the modal. You can paste the Sample JSON or drag-and-drop a crisis file (e.g., Strait of Hormuz closure).
   - Click **Upload & Recalculate**.
   - Explain: *"We just injected a major geopolitical supply shock. The Master Intelligence Pipeline immediately ran all backend engines in a 9-step cascade to compute the new security state."*

---

### **0:30 - 1:15 | Demo Playback & Risk Intelligence**
3. **Demo Mode**:
   - Navigate to `/demo-mode`.
   - Explain: *"For training and simulation, administrators use Demo Mode to step through crisis phases step-by-step."*
   - Click **Next Step** to transition the scenario from Phase 1 (Incident) to Phase 2 (Escalation). Observe the timeline update.
4. **Risk Intelligence**:
   - Navigate to `/risk-intelligence`.
   - Point out the **National Risk Score** (now spiking into the Critical Red Zone) and the route risk vectors.
   - Explain: *"Our Risk Engine has automatically detected shipping route hazards, spiking insurance premiums, and localized refinery disruptions."*
5. **Scenario Simulator**:
   - Navigate to `/scenario-simulator`.
   - Explain: *"This page allows security teams to model changes to the current crisis (e.g., increasing shipping delay or oil price shocks)."*
   - Click **Run Simulation** to compute and graph the new outcome.

---

### **1:15 - 2:00 | Economic Impact & Operational Optimization**
6. **Economic Impact**:
   - Navigate to `/economic-impact`.
   - Explain: *"The Economic Engine maps the physical supply deficit to macroeconomic data."*
   - Point out the predicted **CPI Inflation Spike**, **GDP Drag**, and the **Domestic Fuel Price Delta** (Petrol/Diesel increase) if no mitigation is taken.
7. **Procurement Optimizer**:
   - Navigate to `/procurement-optimizer`.
   - Point out the original high-risk routes vs. the AI-optimized procurement plan.
   - Explain: *"The Procurement Optimizer automatically reroutes cargo contracts away from the Strait of Hormuz to safer, pre-negotiated West African and US Gulf suppliers, minimizing both cost and transit risk."*
   - Click **Optimize** to verify the plan.

---

### **2:00 - 2:30 | Reserve Drawdowns, Compliance & Red Teaming**
8. **SPR Planner**:
   - Navigate to `/spr-planner`.
   - Point out the predicted inventory depletion graph.
   - Explain: *"To bridge the shipping gap, the SPR Planner recommends a calculated release from our Strategic Petroleum Reserves at Vizag and Mangalore."*
   - Click **Generate Drawdown Brief**.
9. **Compliance Shield**:
   - Navigate to `/compliance-shield`.
   - Explain: *"We check every incoming cargo against global legal frameworks. Here, the Compliance Shield flags any tankers failing G7 price caps or lacking valid P&I insurance."*
   - Click **Flag for Review** to log a compliance exception.
10. **Red Team Validator**:
    - Navigate to `/red-team-validator`.
    - Explain: *"The Red Team Validator stress-tests our optimized plan against adversarial moves (e.g., cyberattacks on pipeline SCADA nodes or a secondary chokepoint blockade)."*

---

### **2:30 - 3:00 | Executive Brief, Decision & Governance**
11. **AI Action Brief**:
    - Navigate to `/action-brief`.
    - Explain: *"The brief generator consolidates all technical telemetry into a clear, executive summary with prioritized tasks."*
    - Click **Export Brief** to download the text brief locally.
    - Click **Executive Board** to route to the boardroom.
12. **Executive Board**:
    - Navigate to `/executive-decision-board`.
    - Explain: *"Here, ministers and directors vote on the proposed drawdowns and procurement actions."*
    - Cast votes and click **Record Official Decision**.
13. **Audit Logs**:
    - Navigate to `/audit-logs`.
    - Explain: *"Every single action—scenarios activated, custom feeds uploaded, and boardroom votes—is secured in our audit log for complete accountability."*
    - Click **Refresh Logs** to show the final trail of our crisis response.
